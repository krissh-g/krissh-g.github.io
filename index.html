Biography

Krishnendu Ghosh has joined the Department of Electrical and Electronics Communication Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur as a Ph.D. Research Scholar in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Prabir Kumar Biswas. Before that, he has received M. Tech degree in Visual information processing and embedded systems from the same institute in 2022.

His research interests cover computer vision and deep learning.
